Smart Food AI is built as a distributed intelligence ecosystem that connects agricultural production, storage infrastructure, logistics networks, and consumer demand using AI-driven predictive coordination.

The architecture is designed around one central philosophy:

## 3. Data Flow Narrative

1. Crop harvest data is uploaded through farmer mobile interface.
2. Environmental data is streamed from IoT storage sensors.
3. Satellite imagery validates crop health.
4. ANNAPURNA AI evaluates spoilage probability.
5. Market demand AI forecasts optimal destination.
6. Distribution optimizer generates logistics recommendation.
7. Blockchain records traceability.
8. Stakeholders receive actionable alerts.
